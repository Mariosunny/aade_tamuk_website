About Us

The American Association of Drilling Engineers at Texas A&M University-Kingsville was founded in September 2016 by Charoenpong Songdechakraiwut.
The organization is operating under the umbrella of the Central Texas Chapter.

Mission

We strive for excellence by offering a forum where our members can share their knowledge in drilling technology,
and a channel to further their future professional careers in the O&G industry.

Vision

To be an organization that benefits Texas A&M University-Kingsville the drilling industry, and the future of our members.

Values

To promote professionalism and respect within the drilling industry and the engineering community
